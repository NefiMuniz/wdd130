Overview

Purpose

This website aims to provide information about rafting tours and adventures, catering to adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Audience

The target audience includes:

  • Individuals and groups interested in outdoor activities.
  • Adventure sports enthusiasts.
  • Eco-tourism Lovers.

Scenario Questions:

1. Are you interested in outdoor adventure activities, such as water rafting?

Answer: Yes, our target audience is interested in outdoor adventure activities, especially water rafting.

2. Do you enjoy spending time in nature and participating in adrenaline-pumping experiences?

Answer: Absolutely! Our audience enjoys spending time outdoors and seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures like water rafting.

3. Are you planning a group vacation and looking for exciting activities to enjoy together?

Answer: Yes, many families and groups are looking for exciting activities to enjoy together, making them a perfect fit for our water rafting tours.
